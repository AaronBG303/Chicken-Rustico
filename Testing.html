Chicken Rustico

A pretty quick, delicious Chicken Rustico recipe!

about 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (10 oz.) package fresh baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup dry red wine or beef broth
  • 3 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 2 medium roma tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 (14 oz.) can artichoke hearts, drained
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp. oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic to the pan and saute until lightly brown.
  3. Add chicken and saute for 5 minutes or until browned on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  4. Heat 1 tbsp. oil in the same skillet until hot. Add minced garlic to the pan and saute until lightly brown.
  5. Add mushrooms and saut√© for 5 minutes, or until mushrooms are tender. Stir in broth, wine, and mustard. Return chicken to the skillet. Add tomatoes and artichoke hearts. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and cook for 10 minutes, or until the chicken is no longer pink in the center.
  6. Combine cornstarch with 1 tbsp. cold water. Stir into the sauce and bring to a boil. Cook, stirring, over high heat for about 1 minute or until the sauce thickens. Serve over a bed of cooked orzo pasta.
